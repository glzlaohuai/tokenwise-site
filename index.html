Private by design隐私优先，数据不出本机

Tokenly reads the local session files your AI tools already write — and computes everything on your Mac. Your usage and conversations are never uploaded to our servers. In fact, we don't run a server that could receive them. Tokenly 读取 AI 工具本就写在本机的会话文件，并在你的 Mac 上完成全部计算。你的用量与对话绝不上传到我们的服务器——事实上，我们根本没有能接收它们的服务器。

Networking is used only for the push channel you set up yourself. 网络仅用于你自己配置的推送渠道。
