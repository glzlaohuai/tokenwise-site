FAQ

Which tools are supported?

Claude Code, Codex, OpenCode, and Gemini.

Is my data safe?

All processing happens locally; nothing is uploaded. See the Privacy Policy.

How do I get started?

On first launch, an onboarding guide walks you through authorizing each source. In Settings → Sources, grant access to the relevant folders and Tokenly will read your local usage. It lives in your menu bar — click the icon for an overview.

How do I set quota alerts?

In Settings → Quota & Alerts, set a daily token limit per project or globally. You’ll be alerted as you approach or exceed it, and alerts can be pushed to a channel you configure.

How do I uninstall?

Quit the app and delete Tokenly from your Applications folder; local data is removed with it.

常见问题

支持哪些工具？

Claude Code、Codex、OpenCode、Gemini。

我的数据安全吗？

所有统计都在本机完成，不上传任何数据。详见 隐私政策。

如何开始使用？

首次启动会引导你逐个授权数据源；在「设置 → 数据源」中授权对应目录后，即可读取本地用量。Tokenly 常驻菜单栏，点击图标即可查看概览。

如何设置额度预警？

在「设置 → 额度 & 预警」为项目或全局设置每日 token 上限，接近或超限时会提醒你，也可推送到你自己配置的渠道。

如何卸载？

退出 App 后，从「应用程序」中删除 Tokenly 即可，本地数据随之清除。
